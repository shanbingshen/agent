# Arthra Knowledge Assets

`knowledge` stores business knowledge assets. RAG engine code lives in
`packages/rag`; agents must call RAG APIs instead of reading files or vector
stores directly.

## Layout

```text
raw/          Human-maintained source files such as PDF, DOCX, Markdown and TXT.
processed/    Parsed documents and chunks generated by ingestion jobs.
metadata/     Human-maintained source metadata.
manifests/    Ingestion runs, versions and index lineage.
```

## Domain Scope

Knowledge is split by agent domain. Agent configs declare which domains they may
retrieve from.

```text
raw/shared/       Shared standards and energy basics.
raw/ems/          EMS operation and optimization knowledge.
raw/power/        Transformer, power quality and demand knowledge.
raw/compressor/   Manuals, faults and maintenance knowledge.
raw/carbon/       Accounting and emission factor knowledge.
raw/customer/     Customer-specific private knowledge.
```

Customer knowledge must not be mixed with shared or public domain knowledge.
Customer identity, equipment registry and operational telemetry belong in
PostgreSQL or time-series storage; contracts, reports and operating procedures
may be indexed through RAG with tenant and factory filters.
